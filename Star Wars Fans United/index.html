Welcome to Star Wars Fans United!

Star Wars Fans United is a group for fans of Star Wars to meet up and discuss their favorite franchise. All fans are welcome, no matter your level of knowledge!

Many of our current members are fans of the Old Jedi Order circa 3600 BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin, for those unaware),
so many discussions will center around the galaxy at this time.

We hope you make new friends and have fun!
